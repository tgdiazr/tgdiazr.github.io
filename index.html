About me

I’m a data scientist and software developer who helps organizations make better decisions with their data — and builds the tools that put those decisions to work.

Today I work as a systems analyst and programmer, developing web applications and data systems used across a large public-sector organization. I’m strong with C#/.NET, Python, SQL, Power BI, machine learning, and the major cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud), and I work in both English and Spanish.

I started my career in research as a computational scientist, where I built machine-learning models and ran large-scale simulations on supercomputers to predict how materials behave. That work produced seven peer-reviewed papers, a book chapter, and a granted patent — and it’s the same analytical rigor I bring to business and engineering problems today.
